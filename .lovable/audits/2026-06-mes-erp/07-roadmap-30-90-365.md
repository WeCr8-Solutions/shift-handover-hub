### Theme 5 — "Anti-features" (ongoing)

Explicitly **say no** to these throughout the year, even when prospects ask:

- General Ledger / AP / AR
- Full WMS / RF picking
- Full HRIS / benefits / payroll *processing* (we export, we don't run payroll)
- CRM / sales-automation
- E-commerce / order-management for direct-to-consumer
- Field-service / installed-base management
- EDI primitives (use SPS Commerce / Cleo as partners)
- Project accounting / PSA
- Process-industry MES (batches, recipes, regulatory submissions)

Each "no" is a feature: it keeps the product focused on Manufacturing Workforce Execution.

## Sequencing Notes

- Items #7 (per-WO labor minutes) → #8 (time clock) → #9 (payroll export) → #24 (cost variance) form a single dependency chain. Sequence them in that order.
- MTConnect (#4 90-day) → OPC-UA (#22 90-day) → Predictive maintenance (Q1 2027) form another chain that needs 6+ months of corpus.
- Cutting-tool inventory (#5 90-day) → Tooling Hero connector (#17 90-day) — connector is cheap *after* inventory exists.
- Finite scheduler (Q4 2026) → Gantt (#10 90-day, prep) → AI auto-generate schedule (Q4 2026) — Gantt becomes the visualization for the scheduler.
- ECN (#2 90-day) → PFMEA/Control Plan (Q1 2027) — PFMEA only makes sense once ECN exists to manage change.

Sequencing is the difference between a roadmap that ships and one that doesn't.
