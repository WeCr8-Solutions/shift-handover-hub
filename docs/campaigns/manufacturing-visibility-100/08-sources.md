# 08 — Sources and Research Notes

## Inspiration

The ranking-list growth pattern is well-documented in early-2000s tech media (Silicon Alley Reporter's "Silicon Alley 100" being the most cited example). The lesson — *people promote media that gives them, challenges, or excludes them from status* — is what we're applying here, not the format itself.

## Source material

This campaign was seeded by an uploaded planning document (`manufacturing_100_agent_plan.zip`, received June 2026). The original document is preserved in version control as the seed; the curated, JobLine-voiced docs in this folder supersede it for any operational decision.

## Public reference sources for candidate research

- LinkedIn search by job title + content engagement.
- YouTube manufacturing/CNC creator landscape.
- Trade publications (Modern Machine Shop, MoldMaking Technology, Production Machining, Aerospace Manufacturing & Design).
- Conference rosters (IMTS, Westec, Hannover Messe, AeroDef).
- Industry podcasts (MakingChips, The Manufacturing Show, Lights Out).
- Trade-school and apprenticeship directories.
- Public company manufacturing leadership rosters.
