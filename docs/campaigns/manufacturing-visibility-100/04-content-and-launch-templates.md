# 04 — Content and Launch Templates

Reusable copy for the campaign. Edit per channel; keep the tone direct, credit-forward, and free of hype.

## Social — nominations open

> Manufacturing has thousands of hidden builders doing world-class work. We're recognizing 100 of them.
>
> Nominations are open for the **Manufacturing Visibility 100** — programmers, operators, shop owners, educators, and software builders pushing the industry forward.
>
> Nominate someone (including yourself): jobline.ai/manufacturing-100

## Email — nominee notification (shortlisted)

> Subject: You've been shortlisted for the Manufacturing Visibility 100
>
> Hi {first_name},
>
> {nominator_name} nominated you for the Manufacturing Visibility 100 — JobLine.ai's annual list of the 100 people and organizations pushing modern manufacturing forward.
>
> You've been shortlisted in the **{category}** category. Before we publish, we'd like to confirm a few details and offer you a chance to share anything we should reference.
>
> Reply here, or use this link to update your entry: {link}
>
> If you'd prefer not to be listed, just reply "remove" and we'll honor it within 7 days, no questions asked.
>
> — The JobLine.ai editorial team

## Email — winner notification

> Subject: You're on the Manufacturing Visibility 100
>
> Hi {first_name},
>
> You've been named to the inaugural Manufacturing Visibility 100, ranked **#{rank}** in **{category}**.
>
> Attached is your share kit: a personalized badge graphic, suggested social copy, and a link to your entry on the public list.
>
> Thank you for the work you do. The industry is better for it.

## Badge spec

- 1200×1200 PNG, transparent background.
- Centered category mark, rank in monospace, name in display serif, "Manufacturing Visibility 100 · 2026" wordmark.
- Two variants: light and dark.
- Filename: `mfg-100-2026-{slug}.png`.

## "Who did we miss" follow-up post

A week after launch, publish a short piece inviting omissions and corrections. This is the productive controversy loop — keep it generous in tone, not defensive.
