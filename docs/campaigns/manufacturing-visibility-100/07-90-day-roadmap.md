# 07 — 90-Day Roadmap

## Days 1–14 — Foundation

- Ship `/manufacturing-100` landing + `/manufacturing-100/methodology` + `/manufacturing-100/nominate`.
- Backend table live; nominations writeable; consent enforced.
- Publish kickoff blog post.
- Add cross-links on `/talent` and `/learn`.
- Soft-announce in JobLine.ai newsletter and on LinkedIn.

## Days 15–30 — Nomination drive

- 3× LinkedIn posts/week soliciting nominations.
- Personal outreach to 50 known industry voices asking them to nominate peers (not themselves).
- Founder/editorial post on "why manufacturing needs more recognition."
- Internal dashboard to triage incoming nominations.

## Days 31–60 — Research and shortlist

- Editorial + agent-assisted research expands the candidate pool to 150–300.
- Provisional scores assigned; editor begins top-100 review.
- Shortlisted nominees receive confirmation emails and a chance to update their entries.
- Badge graphics designed; share kits drafted.

## Days 61–80 — Editorial close

- Top 100 finalized with human editor sign-off on every entry.
- Category leads finalized.
- Sponsor/media kit drafted (no rank sponsorship — see methodology).
- Pre-launch teaser post.

## Days 81–90 — Launch

- Publish the list with the main article, category pages, and individual share kits.
- Day-after: "who did we miss" companion post.
- Outreach to featured shops (demo) and operators (Talent).
- Begin alumni-list build for Year 2.

## After launch — Year 2 prep

- Track outcomes: demo requests, Talent signups, OAP/GCA traffic, press pickups, sponsor inquiries.
- Year 2 adds up/down indicators, alumni network, sponsor-backed category awards, and a virtual award event.
