## Notes
- Always lead the second follow-up with a real update. "Still raising" is not an update.
- Asking for a referral on the way out is the highest-ROI line in the whole sequence — passes routinely turn into warm intros elsewhere.
- Log every reply (even no-replies) in your tracker so you don't accidentally re-pitch the same partner in 12 months.
