# JobLine.ai — One-Pager

**Digital expeditor & smart shift handoff system for small and mid-size CNC job shops.**
Replacing clipboards, whiteboards, and tribal knowledge with real-time work order intelligence, structured shift handoffs, and an operator credential pipeline.

---

**Problem.** 65% of US manufacturers still run the shop floor on paper or spreadsheets (NIST, 2025). Shift handoffs lose context, NCRs recur, quotes are re-built from scratch, and tribal knowledge walks out the door with every retirement. Legacy MES costs $100K+ and takes 90–180 days to deploy — out of reach for the SMB shops that make up the long tail of US precision manufacturing.

**Product.** Four integrated layers, one platform:

- **JobLine.ai (core)** — Work orders, shift handoffs, NCR/quality holds, machine context, supervisor dashboard, AI Planning Assistant. Deploys in <5 days.
- **GCA — G-Code Academy** — Browser-based CNC training (Lathe, Mill, GD&T, Fanuc/Haas/Siemens) with $12 verifiable certificates.
- **OAP — Operator Acceptance Program** — 7-section mentor-verified machine qualification covering safety, measuring, tooling, controls, machine OAK, quality.
- **Talent** — GCA + OAP credentials auto-sync to portable operator profiles. Employers search by machine, controller, cert level.

**Why now.** (1) CNC workforce retirement — avg machinist is 55+, undocumented knowledge is bleeding out. (2) No portable digital CNC credential exists — window to set the standard is open. (3) SMB owners who dismissed software 3 years ago are now actively shopping post-Industry 4.0 messaging from AWS, NVIDIA, Siemens — but nothing exists under $100K.
