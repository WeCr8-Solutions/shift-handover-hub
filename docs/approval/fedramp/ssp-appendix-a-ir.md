### **IR-1** Policy and Procedures
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The organization has a documented Incident Response Plan (IRP) that outlines the procedures for incident response. This plan is referenced in all relevant documents and is reviewed annually.  
**Responsible Role:** Security Officer  
**Test Method:** Review of the IRP document and confirmation from the Security Officer.

### **IR-2** Incident Response Training
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Annual training on incident response procedures is conducted for all personnel, including tabletop exercises. Training records are maintained and reviewed annually.  
**Responsible Role:** HR Manager  
**Test Method:** Review of training records and confirmation from the HR Manager.

### **IR-3** Incident Response Testing
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Annual tabletop tests are conducted to evaluate the effectiveness of the incident response plan. Results are documented in the IRP and reviewed annually.  
**Responsible Role:** Security Officer  
**Test Method:** Review of test results and confirmation from the Security Officer.

### **IR-4** Incident Handling
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The IRP defines a 6-phase lifecycle for incident handling: Preparation, Detection, Containment, Eradication, Recovery, and Post-Incident. Critical incidents are responded to within a 4-hour SLA.  
**Responsible Role:** Security Officer  
**Test Method:** Review of the IRP document and confirmation from the Security Officer.

### **IR-5** Incident Monitoring
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Supabase monitoring, Vercel alerts, and activity_logs for behavioral anomalies are in place to detect incidents. Slack #security-alerts channel is used for notifications.  
**Responsible Role:** IT Manager  
**Test Method:** Review of monitoring tools and confirmation from the IT Manager.

### **IR-6** Incident Reporting
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** US-CERT/CISA reports are submitted within 1 hour of confirmed incidents, and FedRAMP JAB/AO notifications are made as per FedRAMP IR requirements.  
**Responsible Role:** Security Officer  
**Test Method:** Review of incident reporting records and confirmation from the Security Officer.

### **IR-7** Incident Response Assistance
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The Engineering Lead is the primary contact for incident response, with the CEO as a backup. No IR retainer is currently in place but is planned for Phase 2.  
**Responsible Role:** Engineering Lead  
**Test Method:** Confirmation from the Engineering Lead and CEO.

### **IR-8** Incident Response Plan
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The IRP is maintained at docs/approval/fedramp/incident-response-plan.md, reviewed annually, and distributed to the CEO and Engineering Lead.  
**Responsible Role:** Security Officer  
**Test Method:** Review of the IRP document and confirmation from the Security Officer.

### **IR-10** Integrated Information Security Analysis Team
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The WeCr8 engineering team serves as the ISAT, with external IR firm engagement planned.  
**Responsible Role:** IT Manager  
**Test Method:** Review of the ISAT plan and confirmation from the IT Manager.
