### **PS-1** Policy and Procedures
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The organization has a documented Personnel Security Policy that outlines the procedures for managing personnel security, including access controls, termination, and transfer of responsibilities. This policy is referenced in all relevant documents and training materials.  
**Responsible Role:** CEO + Engineering Lead  
**Test Method:** Review of the Personnel Security Policy document and verification through interviews with key personnel.

### **PS-2** Position Risk Designation
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** All positions with system access are classified as Moderate risk, as per the documented Personnel Security Policy. This classification is based on the responsibilities and potential impact of each role within the organization.  
**Responsible Role:** CEO + Engineering Lead  
**Test Method:** Review of the position descriptions and their associated responsibilities to ensure they align with the Moderate risk designation.

### **PS-3** Personnel Screening
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Background checks (identity + criminal) are required before granting system access. The CEO administers background checks for the Engineering Lead, while the Engineering Lead administers checks for future hires. These checks are documented in the personnel security records.  
**Responsible Role:** CEO + Engineering Lead  
**Test Method:** Review of the background check documentation and verification through interviews with key personnel.

### **PS-4** Personnel Termination
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The organization has a documented procedure for terminating personnel access, which includes disabling accounts within 1 hour of separation, rotating any shared credentials, and recovering assets. This procedure is executed by the Engineering Lead under the supervision of the CEO.  
**Responsible Role:** Engineering Lead + CEO  
**Test Method:** Review of the termination procedures document and verification through interviews with key personnel.

### **PS-5** Personnel Transfer
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The organization has a documented reassignment procedure that includes re-evaluating access need, adjusting RBAC roles before the transfer effective date. This procedure is executed by the Engineering Lead under the supervision of the CEO.  
**Responsible Role:** Engineering Lead + CEO  
**Test Method:** Review of the reassignment procedures document and verification through interviews with key personnel.

### **PS-6** Access Agreements
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** All personnel must sign the Rules of Behavior before gaining system access, which are documented in the Rules of Behavior document. Annual re-acknowledgment is also required. This agreement is enforced during onboarding and through periodic reminders.  
**Responsible Role:** HR + Engineering Lead  
**Test Method:** Review of the signed access agreements and verification through interviews with key personnel.

### **PS-7** External Personnel Security
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Vendors/contractors must sign an NDA and acknowledge the Rules of Behavior before gaining access to the system. No standing access is granted to third parties without proper authorization. This requirement is enforced during onboarding and through regular audits.  
**Responsible Role:** Engineering Lead + CEO  
**Test Method:** Review of the NDAs and acknowledgment forms for external personnel and verification through interviews with key personnel.

### **PS-8** Personnel Sanctions
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The organization has a formal sanction policy in the Personnel Security Policy that outlines disciplinary actions up to termination for security violations. This policy is enforced during regular audits and incident investigations.  
**Responsible Role:** HR + Engineering Lead  
**Test Method:** Review of the sanction policy document and verification through interviews with key personnel.

### **PS-9** Position Descriptions
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** System access responsibilities are documented in position descriptions for the Engineering Lead and CEO, ensuring that each role understands their security responsibilities. These descriptions are reviewed annually to ensure they remain up-to-date.  
**Responsible Role:** HR + Engineering Lead  
**Test Method:** Review of the position descriptions and verification through interviews with key personnel.
