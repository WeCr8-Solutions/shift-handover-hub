### **PE-1** Policy and Procedures
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** WeCr8 Solutions has established a comprehensive set of policies and procedures for physical and environmental protection, which are reviewed and updated regularly. ### **PE-2** Physical Access Authorizations
**Implementation Status:** Inherited  
**Description:** Access to data center facilities is controlled by Supabase/Vercel's physical security teams, which are governed by their SOC 2 Type II controls. Only authorized personnel with valid access credentials (badges, biometric authentication) are permitted entry.  
**Responsible Role:** Supabase/Vercel infrastructure team  
**Test Method:** Review Supabase/Vercel SOC 2 Type II report for relevant PE controls.
