# Responsible Disclosure Policy (Vulnerability Disclosure Program)

## 1. Policy Introduction

WeCr8 Solutions LLC is committed to maintaining the security and integrity of our JobLine platform, a SaaS manufacturing shift-handover and work-order platform. This Responsible Disclosure Policy (VDP) outlines the procedures for reporting vulnerabilities in our systems to ensure they are addressed promptly and securely.

## 2. Scope

### In-Scope Systems
- All production endpoints at `jobline.ai`
- All APIs
- Supabase-backed data

### Out-of-Scope Systems
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Denial of Service (DoS) attacks
- Supabase and Vercel infrastructure (to be reported to their respective vendors)

## 3. How to Report

### Contact Information
Please report any vulnerabilities by contacting our security team at `security@jobline.ai`.

### What to Include in Your Report
When reporting a vulnerability, please include the following information:
- A detailed description of the vulnerability.
- Steps to reproduce the issue.
- An assessment of the potential impact (e.g., data loss, unauthorized access).
- Your contact information for follow-up.

## 4. Our Commitments

### Acknowledgment
We will acknowledge all reports within 5 business days and provide a status update within 30 days.

### Patch Timeline
Critical vulnerabilities will be patched within 30 days of acknowledgment.

### Safe Harbor Statement
Good-faith researchers who follow this policy will not face legal action if they report vulnerabilities in accordance with this VDP.

## 5. Researcher Guidelines

- Do not access or modify any customer data.
- Do not perform Denial of Service (DoS) attacks.
- No public disclosure before the patch is released, following a 90-day coordinated disclosure process.
- Do not use automated scanners against production environments without prior approval.
