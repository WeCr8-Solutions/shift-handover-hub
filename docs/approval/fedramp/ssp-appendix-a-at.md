### **AT-1** Policy and Procedures
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** The organization has a Security Awareness Training program documented in the reference document, which includes policies and procedures for conducting security awareness training. This policy is referenced in all training materials and communicated to all personnel.  
**Responsible Role:** CEO  
**Test Method:** Review of the Security Awareness Training program documentation and verification that it is being followed during annual training sessions.

### **AT-2** Literacy Training and Awareness
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** All personnel receive an annual security awareness training session covering phishing, social engineering, secure coding, and incident response. The training completion status is tracked in a training log maintained by the CEO.  
**Responsible Role:** HR Manager  
**Test Method:** Review of the training log to ensure that all personnel have completed the required annual training.

### **AT-2(2)** Insider Threat Awareness
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Insider threat awareness is included in the annual security awareness training for all personnel. Personnel are trained to recognize and report suspicious behavior. Training completion status is tracked in a training log maintained by the CEO.  
**Responsible Role:** HR Manager  
**Test Method:** Review of the training log to ensure that all personnel have completed the required annual training on insider threat awareness.

### **AT-3** Role-Based Training
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Additional training for privileged role holders (admin, Engineering Lead) includes secure coding, OWASP Top 10, FedRAMP responsibilities, and key management. This training is conducted annually or as needed based on changes in the organization's security requirements.  
**Responsible Role:** HR Manager  
**Test Method:** Review of the training records for privileged role holders to ensure that they have completed the required additional training.

### **AT-4** Training Records
**Implementation Status:** Implemented  
**Description:** Training completion records are maintained by the CEO and stored in a secure HR folder. The minimum retention period is 3 years.  
**Responsible Role:** HR Manager  
**Test Method:** Review of the HR folder to ensure that training completion records are being maintained and retained for at least 3 years.
