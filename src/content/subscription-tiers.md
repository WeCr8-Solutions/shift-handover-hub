## FAQ

# can_change_plans
q: Can I change plans anytime?
a: Yes — you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

# exceed_user_limit
q: What happens if I exceed my user limit?
a: We'll notify you and suggest upgrading to a plan that fits your team size. Enterprise plans include 10 seats and can add more at $12/month per seat.

# free_trial
q: Is there a free trial?
a: Yes — all plans come with a 14-day free trial to start.

# talent_profiles_free
q: Are talent profiles really free?
a: Yes — operator talent profiles are always free, with no trial or subscription required. Build a verified shop-floor profile, earn OAP/GCA badges, and get discovered by hiring shops at no cost.
