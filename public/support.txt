We're building enterprise-grade production management software that small and mid-sized machine shops can actually afford. Every manufacturing team deserves visibility into their operations—not just the companies that can afford $100K+ MES systems.

### Why This Matters

- **12+ million Americans** work in manufacturing
- **$50+ billion lost annually** to poor shift handoffs
- **90% of machine shops** can't afford enterprise MES
- **JobLine.ai** bridges this gap with free/affordable tools

---

## 🔧 The Problem We're Solving

**Shift handoffs in manufacturing are broken.**

Every day, millions of manufacturing workers hand off jobs to the next shift. Critical information gets lost:

| Lost Information | Cost Impact |
|-----------------|-------------|
| Machine condition left unknown | Unplanned downtime |
| Parts count discrepancies | Rework and scrap |
| Quality issues not communicated | Customer complaints |
| Material locations unknown | Search time wasted |
| Special instructions missed | Defective parts |

**Combined annual cost: $50+ billion in the US alone**

JobLine.ai fixes this with structured digital handoffs, real-time visibility, and continuous improvement tracking.
