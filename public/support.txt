## 🎯 Mission Statement

JobLine.ai is democratizing manufacturing technology. We're building enterprise-grade production management software that small and mid-sized machine shops can actually afford. Every manufacturing team deserves visibility into their operations—not just the companies that can afford $100K+ MES systems.

## 🔧 The Problem We're Solving

**Shift handoffs in manufacturing are broken.**

Every day, millions of manufacturing workers hand off jobs to the next shift. Critical information gets lost:
- What machine condition was left?
- Which parts were completed?
- What issues need attention?
- Where are the materials?

This costs the US manufacturing industry an estimated **$50+ billion annually** in lost productivity, rework, and miscommunication.

JobLine.ai fixes this with structured digital handoffs, real-time visibility, and continuous improvement tracking.

## 🏭 Product Capabilities

### Core Features
- **Digital Shift Handoffs**: Structured forms for consistent information transfer
- **Work Order Queue**: Priority-based scheduling with status tracking
